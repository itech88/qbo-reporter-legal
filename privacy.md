Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to the QBO COGS Analytics Reporter ("the Application"), a private internal tool developed for use by a single named business client.
Data Collection
The Application connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API solely to retrieve financial report data including income and cost of goods sold figures. No personal data belonging to end users or customers of the business is collected, stored, or processed beyond what is necessary to generate internal financial reports.
Data Use
All data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used exclusively to produce monthly financial summary reports delivered to the authorized business owner. Data is not sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party under any circumstances.
Data Storage
Financial data is cached locally in a SQLite database on infrastructure controlled by the business or its designated IT administrator. This data is not uploaded to any external server or cloud storage service.
Data Retention
Cached financial data is retained for up to three years to support year-over-year comparison reporting. Data may be deleted at any time at the request of the business owner.
Security
API credentials and OAuth tokens are stored in environment variables and are never committed to version control or transmitted outside of authorized API calls to Intuit's servers.
Contact
For questions about this policy, contact the application administrator directly.
